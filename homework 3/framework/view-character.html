Solair of Astora

Solaire is a Warrior of Sunlight on his own quest to link the flame. He is a strong knight who chose to become undead so that he could search for his own sun.

LocationUndead Burg
CovenentWarriors of Sunlight
Souls1,000
ItemsWhite Sign Soapstone, Humanity x2, Sunlight Straight Sword, Sunlight Shield, Sunlight Talisman, Iron and Sun Set
MerchantNo
Quest
1. You will first meet Solaire in the Undead Burg, at the opposite end of the bridge where the Hellkite Dragon sits.
2. After speaking to him, he can then be found in Anor Londo at the bonfire after the silver knight archers.
